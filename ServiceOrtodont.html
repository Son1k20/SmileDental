Conduct root canal


A root canal is performed when the endodontist removes the infected pulp and nerve in the root of the tooth, cleans and shapes the inside of the root canal, then fills and seals the space. After, your dentist will place a crown on the tooth to protect and restore it.

Sinus Lift


What Is It? A sinus lift is surgery that adds bone to your upper jaw in the area of your molars and premolars. It's sometimes called a sinus augmentation. The bone is added between your jaw and the maxillary sinuses, which are on either side of your nose.

$1400

Impacted Canines


Canine teeth often have the longest root of all teeth in the human mouth and the last to fully erupt and fall into place; often around age 13. An impacted tooth essentially means that it is blocked, stuck, or unable to fully erupt and function properly.
