Dental
X-ray


Dental X-rays help dentists visualize diseases and surrounding tissue that cannot be seen with a simple oral exam. They also help the dentist find and treat dental problems early on, which can help save you money, unnecessary discomfort, and maybe even your life.

$30-$750

Cancer screening


Oral cancer screening is an examination performed by a dentist or doctor to look for signs of cancer or precancerous conditions in your mouth. The goal of oral cancer screening is to identify mouth cancer early, when there is a greater chance for a cure.

$70 to $90

Oral examinations


A dental exam is a checkup of your teeth and gums. Most children and adults should get a dental exam every six months or more often. These exams are important for protecting oral health. Oral health problems can become serious and painful if not treated promptly.

$150
